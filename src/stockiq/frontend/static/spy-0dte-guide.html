Core 0DTE Strategies — click to expand
Long Call
BULLISH
Buy a call, expect SPY to move up fast. Cheapest directional bet, but theta kills you if SPY stalls.
Max Loss
Premium paid
Max Gain
Unlimited
Best entry
9:45–11:00
Strike
ATM or 1 strike OTM
  • Enter after the open settles (avoid first 15 min chop)
  • Only trade with clear bullish catalyst or trend
  • Exit before 2pm — theta accelerates hard after that
  • Avoid buying if VIX is elevated (IV makes it pricey)
Long Put
BEARISH
Buy a put, expect SPY to drop fast. Gap-fill trades or FOMC-reaction fades are common setups.
Max Loss
Premium paid
Max Gain
Large (SPY → 0)
Best entry
After gap-up open
Strike
ATM or 1 strike OTM
  • Gap-fill fade: SPY gaps up big → buy puts if likely to fill
  • Check VIX — low VIX means puts are cheaper to buy
  • Size smaller than calls (market has upward bias)
  • Have a clear invalidation level before entering
Bull Call Spread
BULLISH
Buy a call, sell a higher call. Reduces cost dramatically — the go-to for defined-risk bullish plays.
Max Loss
Net debit paid
Max Gain
Spread width − debit
Example
Buy 580C / Sell 582C
Width
$1–$3 strikes wide
  • Ideal when you expect a moderate move, not a moon shot
  • Use $1–$2 wide spreads for low cost, tight risk/reward
  • Close at 50–75% of max profit — don't hold to expiry
  • The short leg caps your gain but also funds the trade
Bear Put Spread
BEARISH
Buy a put, sell a lower put. Same logic as bull call spread but for downside. Reduces theta exposure.
Max Loss
Net debit paid
Max Gain
Spread width − debit
Example
Buy 578P / Sell 576P
Width
$1–$3 strikes wide
  • Best after a sharp gap-up where mean reversion is likely
  • Look for resistance confluence before entering
  • Exit at 50% profit — don't let theta eat back gains
  • Avoid on strong trending days — trend beats mean reversion
Iron Condor
NEUTRAL
Sell a call spread + sell a put spread. Collect premium, profit if SPY stays in a range all day.
Max Loss
Spread width − credit
Max Gain
Net credit received
Best on
Low VIX, choppy days
Wings
0.10–0.20 delta strikes
  • Enter after 10am when the opening range is set
  • Place strikes outside the expected move (check options chain)
  • Best on low-VIX, no-news days — avoid before FOMC/CPI
  • Close at 50% credit received to lock gains, manage risk
Straddle / Strangle
VOLATILITY
Buy both a call and put. Profit from a big move in either direction. Works on high-catalyst days.
Max Loss
Both premiums
Max Gain
Unlimited (either way)
Best on
FOMC / CPI days
Risk
IV crush kills it
  • Only use when a big move is expected but direction unknown
  • Enter before the catalyst — exit immediately after
  • IV crush after the event will destroy value if SPY stalls
  • Strangle = OTM call + OTM put — cheaper but needs bigger move
0DTE Specific Risks
⚡
Gamma explosion
After 2pm, gamma is so high that a $1 SPY move can double or zero your option. One tick against you = massive loss.
EXTREME
⏱
Theta incineration
The entire time value decays to zero by 4pm. Even if SPY moves your way but slowly — theta can exceed your delta gains.
EXTREME
📉
IV crush after events
After FOMC or CPI, IV collapses. You can be right on direction and still lose because IV drops faster than delta gains.
HIGH
🎯
Pin risk at expiry
If SPY closes exactly at your strike, you face unpredictable assignment risk. Always close positions before 3:50pm.
HIGH
📊
Wide bid/ask at close
OTM options near expiry can have $0.05–$0.30 wide spreads. You lose edge on entry AND exit. Always use limit orders.
HIGH
🧠
FOMO & overtrading
0DTE moves fast. The urge to re-enter after a loss is the #1 account killer. Define max daily loss and stop cold.
EXTREME
Pre-Trade Checklist
Check VIX level VIX <16: buy options. VIX >25: sell premium (condors/spreads).
Check economic calendar FOMC, CPI, NFP = IV crush risk. Know before you trade.
Note premarket gap size and direction Big gap-up → fade candidate. Small gap → trend follow setup.
Wait for the opening range (first 15 min) Mark the 9:30–9:45 high and low. These are key levels for the day.
Define your max loss before entering 0DTE: never risk more than you can see go to zero instantly.
Check delta and cost of target option ATM delta ~0.50. OTM delta <0.30 = needs a bigger move to profit.
Set a hard exit time: no holding past 3:45pm Gamma and pin risk make the last 15 min a coin flip. Just close.
⚠ Warning: 0DTE options can go to zero within minutes. This is educational only, not financial advice.
Most 0DTE traders lose money. Paper trade first. Understand the Greeks before risking real capital.
